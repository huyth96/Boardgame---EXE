How to Play
  • Shuffle the deck and deal the cards to each player.
  • On your turn, draw a card and complete the challenge or effect.
  • Scan the card's QR code for instant meaning, sample sentences, or audio.
  • Earn points, win rewards, and train your English reflexes and memory!
